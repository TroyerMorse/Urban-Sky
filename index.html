Reusable near-space balloons

Urban Sky is developing the first ever reusable 'micro-sat' version of a near-space balloon, armed with sensors that collect high-value Earth observation data over entire cities, serving a multi-billion dollar imagery market and making daily and weekly aerial imagery collection a cost-effective reality.

What is the difference?

Our micro-balloons will collect more frequent and higher-res data (better than satellites), with broader area coverage (better than UAVs and aircraft), all at a cost orders of magnitude cheaper, serving a wide range of markets that include urban planning, insurance, construction, fresh maps for autonomous cars, public safety, roofing, agriculture and land use, and many more.
